Where We Started

Built on the Fundamentals of the Trade

SRS Diamonds was founded in Antwerp in November 2012. In the early years the core business was loose diamonds — small sizes under one carat, mainly Non-Certified rounds in parcels for manufacturers and traders. Those goods sit at the base of the jewellery industry; learning them thoroughly is what set the technical standard for everything that followed.

As the next generation joined, the company expanded into GIA-certified stones across all shapes, rounds and fancies. Stock today ranges from small sizes up to about five carats, with select specials toward the ten-carat mark — growth driven by client trust and by the depth of experience applied to every category we touch.

The timeline below marks the main steps along that path, from first international show to our current headquarters and presence at fairs worldwide.
