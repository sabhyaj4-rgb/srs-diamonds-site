Rishi Jain
Founder & Managing Director

Rishi Jain has spent his entire professional life in the natural diamond trade. His career began at the age of eighteen, driven by a genuine love for diamonds and the industry built around them. Over more than three decades, he has developed one of the most thorough and rounded understandings of the trade available.

Career Timeline
1989–1991
Began his training in the rough diamond industry, learning the complete process from rough assortment through to rough planning.
1992
Transitioned fully into polished diamonds. Studied assortment, grading, buying and pricing, and developed a trained eye for distinguishing well-made polished stones from average or poor quality goods.
Jan 1993
Travelled to Antwerp, Belgium for the first time. Antwerp has been his professional base ever since, while travelling extensively across Europe and globally to deepen his market knowledge and build his personal standing in the trade.
1993–2012
A twenty-four year tenure under a single company, building expertise, relationships and a reputation for precision and integrity across every aspect of the natural diamond trade.
Nov 2012
Founded SRS Diamonds in Antwerp, beginning the next chapter of his career with a clear vision: to supply natural polished diamonds of the highest standard, with the direct personal service that defines the company today.

Rishi's philosophy has always been rooted in something simple: a perfect diamond creates a moment of pure joy for the person who receives it. That belief shapes every decision made at SRS Diamonds.
