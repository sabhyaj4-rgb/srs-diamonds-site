From Source to Client

Six Steps to Certainty

01

Sourcing

Direct relationships with Antwerp cutters and manufacturers. Access to quality goods from the heart of the world's diamond capital.

02

Team Evaluation

Every stone is assessed by multiple trained eyes across our team before it reaches a client — ensuring it meets our exact standards. This collective scrutiny is what separates us.

03

Grading & Sorting

Precise measurement and calibration. Colour and clarity verification. Parcel matching for consistency across your entire order.

04

Specification Matching

We listen to your exact requirements: desired ratios, clarity and colour ranges, size distribution. Then we match our inventory to your specification precisely.

05

Natural Origin Verification

As part of our commitment to supplying only natural polished diamonds, goods are routinely passed through screening equipment in our office as a standard part of our process. We use specialist detection instruments — including the Yehuda Sherlock Holmes and the M-Screen+ — to help confirm that stones are consistent with natural diamond characteristics.

06

Delivery & Support

Insured shipping worldwide. Full documentation and certification where required. An ongoing relationship, we are here after the order too, not just before it.
